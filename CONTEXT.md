# Detran RJ Exam Practice

This context describes the study experience for practicing Detran RJ simulated exam questions.

## Language

**Practice Session**:
A bounded attempt over a selected set of questions, with its own question order, score, and missed-question list.
_Avoid_: quiz run, exam run, study flow

**Mistake Review**:
A Practice Session built from questions the learner has previously missed, prioritized by weakest performance. Once a question has been missed, it remains eligible for Mistake Review, but becomes lower priority as the learner improves on it.
_Avoid_: error list, wrong answers mode, corrections

**Post-Session Review**:
A review shown after a Practice Session ends, containing the questions missed in that just-finished session.
_Avoid_: Mistake Review, corrections screen

**Question Performance**:
The learner's accuracy for a specific question, calculated from confirmed attempts as correct answers divided by total answers for that question.
_Avoid_: question score, confidence
